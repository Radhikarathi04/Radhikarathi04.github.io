About Me

I'm a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Engineer based in London, combining design thinking with technical implementation to create intuitive, user-centered digital products.

With 2+ years of professional experience at Marks & Spencer, I've worked on customer-facing e-commerce applications including homepage features, product detail pages, and checkout flows. I specialize in React, Next.js, and TypeScript, bringing designs to life with clean, performant code.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the gap between design and development. I don't just implement designs, I understand the "why" behind them. My background in both disciplines allows me to collaborate effectively with design teams, advocate for users, and deliver polished interfaces that balance aesthetics with functionality.

I follow a T-shaped learning approach: deep expertise in frontend development and UI/UX design, with working knowledge of backend systems and iOS development. This versatility helps me understand the full product lifecycle and make better design decisions.

When I'm not coding or designing, I enjoy sketching, practicing yoga, and reading. I believe in continuous learning and staying curious about emerging design trends and technologies.
